Topic 2 : Operators and Conditional Statement

Operators -

It is used to perform some operations on data.

1. ARTITHEMETIC OPERATION
  • Addition (a+b), subtraction (a-b), multiplication (a*b), divide (a/b)
  • Modulus (a%b)
  • Exponentiation (a to power b (2 to power 3 -> 2*2*2))
  • Increment (a++ / ++a)
  • Decrement (a-- / --a)
2. ASSIGNMENT OPERATION
  • = (a=b)
  • += (a+=b)
  • -= (a-=b)
  • *= (a*=b)
  • %= (a%=b)
  • **= (a**b)
3. COMPARISON OPERATION
  • == (a==b)
  • != (a!=b)
  • === (a===b) -> chek equal to and it type
  • !== (a!==b) -> chek not equal to and it type
3. LOGICAL OPERATION
  • && -> Logical AND
  • || -> Logical OR
  • ! -> Logical NOT
